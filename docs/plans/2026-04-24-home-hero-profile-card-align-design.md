# Home Hero Profile Card Alignment Design

## Goal

Move the profile card in the home hero upward on desktop so it supports the main headline earlier in the reading flow.

## Decision

Top-align the hero columns on large screens and give the right column a light top offset rather than leaving the card bottom-aligned.

## Rationale

- The current bottom alignment makes the profile card feel delayed and visually secondary.
- Moving it upward improves first-glance comprehension for hiring managers by surfacing identity and current role sooner.
- A small offset preserves the editorial composition and avoids making the right column feel mechanically pinned to the top edge.

## Scope

- Desktop hero alignment only.
- No copy changes.
- No mobile layout changes.
